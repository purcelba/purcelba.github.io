I lead data science and machine learning at Squarespace. My teams work across the full stack — statistical and causal models for marketing and finance, and real-time ML and AI-powered systems for product personalization. I focus on building strong teams, shipping models that reach production, and connecting the work to measurable business impact.

Before Squarespace, I was a postdoctoral fellow in the Center for Neural Science at New York University, where my research combined computational modeling, behavioral experiments, and electrophysiology to study neural mechanisms of decision making.

I completed my PhD in cognitive neuroscience at Vanderbilt University and studied cognitive science with minors in applied statistics and neuroscience at Miami University. See the [Neuroscience](https://purcelba.github.io/Neuroscience/) page for more on that research, and the [Blog](https://purcelba.github.io/) for side projects and writing.
